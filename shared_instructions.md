# Background

This agency specializes in comprehensive company intelligence gathering and analysis. The primary focus is collecting detailed information about companies from any public source and generating actionable business insights.

## Target Companies

- Startups from YC, TechStars, and other accelerators
- Companies from VC portfolio pages
- Individual company websites
- Company directories and catalogs (up to 20 companies)

## Data Collection Focus

1. **Basic Info**: Name, website, phone, description
2. **Fundraising**: Stage, round size, investors, valuation
3. **Company History**: How they started, pivots, key milestones
4. **User Feedback**: Reddit discussions, reviews, pain points
5. **Financials**: Revenue, growth, unit economics
6. **Business Opportunities**: Score (0-100) and tailored proposals

## Output Requirements

- Structured report for each company
- CSV file saved to `mnt/` directory at repository root
- Companies ranked by Business Opportunity Score (0-100)
- CSV_ROW format for easy data extraction
